About

Come join us from September 12th to September 13th at Rensselaer Polytechnic Insitute to partake in our first ever Leadership Summit! The conference will include leadership training based on communication, team building, and public speaking.

You can register for any of our 3 options! The $40 registration fee includes lodging for two nights, food, and swag, the $35 registration fee includes lodging for a night, food, and swag, and lastly, the $25 registration fee includes all of the aforementioned amenities with the exception of lodging. We hope to see you there!
